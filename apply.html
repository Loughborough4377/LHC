How to Apply

Applications for Loughborough Housing are managed through the City of Kingston’s Social Services.

📝

Start Your Application

Click the button below to download and fill out the official Social Services form for Rent-Geared-to-Income assistance.

Download Application Form (PDF)

Note: This will open the City of Kingston's official document.

1. Fill Out

Complete the RGI assistance form provided by the City of Kingston.

2. Submit

Deliver or email the completed form to the Housing & Social Services office.

3. Waitlist

Once processed, you will be placed on the centralized waiting list.
